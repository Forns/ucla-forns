About

Andrew Forney is a graduate student presently in the UCLA computer science department, studying artificial intelligence under the advisement of Dr. Judea Pearl and the Cognitive Systems Laboratory. He attended Loyola Marymount University for his undergraduate career where he studied computer science, psychology, and pure mathematics, and performs continued research with professors in LMU's computer science and psychology departments. He is also a member of the board for the Immersive Education Initiative (iED West Coast Chapter) where he serves to organize conferences and examine the leading technology in immersive electronic devices and applications.
