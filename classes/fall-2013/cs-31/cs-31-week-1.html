Introduction: The Discussion Section

Discussion sections are designed as review, practice, and concept expansion sessions where we examine the topics of the week's lectures in greater detail. We'll generally follow this agenda:

  • One Minute of Awkward Silence (I wait idly until someone asks a question about the course, homework, or anything)

  • Review of Monday's Lecture Topics (Definitions, explanations, examples, group coding, etc.)

  • Short Break

  • Review of Wednesday's Lecture Topics (Definitions, explanations, examples, group coding, etc.)

  • Lingering Questions / Concerns or Homework Hand-back

What is a development environment?

Development Environment
A collection of your chosen tools, software components, compilers, editors, etc. that you use to develop software.

Once you have installed your preferred compiler, you're ready to start coding.
